It Will Take Time

October 7, 2020

If any one is reading this blog right now and are constantly checking on new updates and feel disappointed, I’m sorry but I have nothing of value to say on a regular basis. I derive value on what I find valuable and don’t find my words worthy of paper at this moment. I have been going through life as a test of one’s ability in action and I think I am actually getting good at it with an equal realization of my stupidity and inability. Humility is important in life.

Meditations have showed me that wisdom is an experienced man’s faculty. I was thinking of writing about education and had written about six thousand words but realized after a few hours that it wasn’t as real as I thought it would be. I am going to write about it but in a way a only a wise man would.

Thank you for reading. If anyone’s reading.
