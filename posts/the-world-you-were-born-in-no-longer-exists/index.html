The moments have passed at a play. I only have the wisdom age can bring. There are times when I remember those old days of plum, innocent youth and wonder where it has all gone. It has changed so much these past few years. Some things I don’t even recognize. Sometimes I don’t recognize myself.

Some nights long while ago, when my grandmother was still alive I thought of things. I thought of death. Of old age. Of being rich and famous. Of being smart and handsome. I seem to be living through that age. Without any of those adjectives. Can’t help but feel like a reject sometimes.

But in the overall, I can’t complain much. It is just the standards you set and talk about never seem to meet actual progress. Maybe it is the grandeur of fiction. Maybe it is that I will go nowhere. These thoughts haunt me from time to time. They are the darkest of all thoughts.

Something has ruined me. What it is I cannot tell. I live in a hyper real world. The hyper reality surrounds me from head to toe. I think like a didact, but go through it like a slave. I think that I think but all of intelligence seems to come from nowhere, leading to nothing. Might as well be a closet nhilist at this point.

But the old days huh. The optimism. The color. The candor. To think what I could be now if I started then and all those things. All of those that take you nowhere.

Who else has had thoughts like these? Who else wants an out? I seem to be digging a tunnel to rid myself of this prison. This is merely a front.

There are some very spiritually disgusting things that I have done. Things I am ashamed of. Things I wouldn’t want to be caught doing. Some know my secrets. Most never will. I hope it stays that way forever.

There are mistakes you make as a child. If you make a mistake now the penance seems to be more than ten fold. But I will keep making a lot of mistakes. You will too. Human, all to Human.

Wandering through life as a Eighteen, nineteen year old. There are a few things I would like to share to all of us lost souls out there. These I have learnt not to do. I don’t prescribe what to do. Only what not to do.

Advice:

Do not keep ink records of yourself on the internet. The internet is written in ink. It will never go away. You will do stupid things every once in a while. Do not put it on the internet. There should be no record of you growing up, except what you keep yourself. (I am talking with a very philosophical angle here. The technical side might come in another post.) Do not keep a record by doing anything stupid. Get a website and start a blog. Your’s truly is an example.

Do not think too much about the opposite sex. Do not watch porn. Porn will construct such a reality in your brain that you will be fucked for life. Do not go there.

Once you say something. You can never unsay it.

Do not construct an identity around smoking, drinking or philandering.

Do not be a nerd.

Do not say that you are going to do something before doing it.

Do not care much about money if you are rich or have generational wealth.

Do not care about politics and political issues. You are a child. Your opinions don’t matter. Shut up.

Do not take history for granted.

And read Uncle Ted.

You will be molded by technology rather than the other way around. Stop the junk.

I claim to be nobody. But these things I know and will vouch for till the end. I have learned some of these things the hard way.

The world you were born in doesn’t exist.
