Making A New Home At Home

August 8, 2020

I have decided to permanently move back to my home and possibly make my old home my new home for quite some time. I will probably enroll in one of the local colleges for a Economics degree and then do something on the side. My pursuit of a foreign education is over for now. The whole rigorous motif of going to some foreign place or a shithole like Kathmandu would be an unwise decision with these places showing pretty fragile outlooks. It is now time to settle here and have a go at it in my own backyard. I will probably start a library in my name here. It’s the least I can do. We’ll see.
