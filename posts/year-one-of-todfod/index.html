In a couple of weeks todfod will be an year old. It has been a simple, quarantined year so far. I have told you some about my life updates.

At first todfod was thought of as a vessel for updating on life and other pursuits. It has done so wonderfully albeit things have been a bit vague. The theme has been switched up to add categories, SSL isn’t supported and there are images. WOW.

But todfod was secretly intended to be a scholastic pursuit where I would post things and writings a little above instagram styled update posts. I have failed in that regard for the simple reason that I don’t know much. I am learning but for all the beads I find, I can’t string them together to mold anything of value that you might not have thought about.

Todfod hasn’t met this secret standard. It will be changed. I know teenagers writing about smoking, love and edgy posts aren’t that interesting to many if not all. I am also pretty sure no more than five people put this address in their address bar often. But I truly don’t care much about the audience as much as I care about the craft.

Todfod isn’t going to end. Not anytime soon. But hopefully the posts will be somewhat interesting. The metric for interesting is what I find interesting. Right now it is all a gaff.

Thank you for reading and welcome to the second year.
