I Remember

August 5, 2020

One day while playing around in one of my previous schools before St. Xaviers, I hit my head in one of the lower edges of a slide and had to get a few stiches. I just happened to remember that moment today. These remembrances of things past haven’t been a rare occuring these past few months. . It is as if the place I came back to had a few tapes to show me of me and now that I remember these moments, the heart fills up with a shuddering uneasiness and a hint of vague nostalgia.

I have always been pretty quite. Not amongst my close friends but in general. I am quite fond of my quiteness. People who talk too much generally have lots to talk but nothing much to say. But sometimes I think I may have been a little too quite. I have always seen that it is the quite ones you should watch out for but sometimes it can make you suffer. Really suffer.

One of my earliest memories of life is of me passing a small irrigation canal on the way from my mother’s parents house to my house. I remember the splash of water when I put my feet in it and I remember tirelessly crossing it to get home as soon as possible. I remember that I was with other children, possibly friends of mine but then I really cannot tell. A distant memory from early childhood is a pretty faded one. Sometimes I even question it regarding its legitimacy. Was it a dream or a memory?

I remember the first day I went to highschool. I remember that one vivdly. I remember the excess of that place. A man suited to my humble tastes and expenses finds himself pretty out of place in a place of such excess, such ‘architecture’, such self proclaimed superiority. It is now quite disappointing to realize that I let the same excess dictate my tastes. I especially remember the people who worked there. I remember those guys vividly. I hate that place.

My Grandmother’s amusement while she tells my childhood stories are irritating but fun. I spent a good deal of my infancy with my grandmother and grandfather and she tries to tell me everything about it. I hardly remember any of it since I was so young and I act like I am not listening to what she has to say but I secretly wish to go back. There is a deep longing to go back but such dreams only a madman dreams of. That is what everyone has had me believe.

I remember much but this is all that I’ll write about for now. Writing about myself is proving to be really difficult.
