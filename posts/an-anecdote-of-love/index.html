Oh! how the times whisper. The gentle breezes form and swindle. Into bouquets of wonderous petals. The hours at hand are precious and ever so giving, ever so taking. Her eyes are of pearls made, her skin - the touch of snow.

I remember being struck without a warning, the ever flooding dam of love burst wide open without stop. The first stairs took to holding hands, slowly but surely to sharing bodies. And how powerful was it to the heart I later realized, my viceful fortitudes curtailed the view. I regret not being completly one with her touch. But the regret poises no answers today.

And it took to more mundane heights but high in their spirits they were. The thoughts we had to share, the feelings we had to awake. All coddled up in little boxes somewhere away.

Part of me wishes back those things but the bigger part wishes to forget its ways. To decline would be monstrous. But love turns you into one. No matter what they say.

And one night as it snows and rains and storms of all sizes plunder everything away. I write in a daring prose to seek to ravage her my way. A release of the shadow? A release of honesty bottled away? She doesn’t like it. I won’t ask forgiveness.

But time has yet passed more and her memory without externalities fades to a corner. I can only hope that she will remember me, She can only hope I will remember her.

Written and added to after a serious heartbreak with a maiden.
