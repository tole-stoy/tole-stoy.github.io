Tobacco and Tea

May 15, 2021

As I write these words there is a faint nicotine buzz in my head. Tobbacco can be fun but to be addicted to it would be quite a distaste for the body. But in moderation everything is alright. In moderation.

As I write this post I am in a vast expanse of tea plants and trees. The air is fresh. It smells of tea. There is a big beautiful tree across this small gentle stream and it is quite amazing.

Man is always best fit and optimal in nature. And to think that this place is only a few kilometeres jog from my home is quite amazing. The birds holler around me and there is no human nearby. Just as I like it sometimes.

The situation with the virus has escalated to new heights and everything is closed down again. The government has failed and so have the people. Some people I knew fell like flies from this and there is no end in sight. What will become of us? What will save us?

Human might and genius are probably the best bets. But until then….. I don’t know.

As the vast and complicated systems of nations, economy and technology have shown themselves to be quite fragile I wonder if any work might be done by our overlords to change course of these subjects. Knowing their competence, this seems foolhardy a thought.

But enough about systems and governments. This was initially about the feels of being with nature and the tea leaves. And nature is the medicine I prescribe to the people feeling lost or betrayed by all things. Remember if it ends, you are at its mercy.
