Politicians:

Politicians never lie. They can never lie. Democracy protects us from politicians. Politicians are democratic. They are fighting for us. They built the bridges. They are fighting to get us vaccinated and healthy. They want the best for our children. They don’t engage in covert malaise, treachery, treason and can still keep a happy face. They are meant to rule us. We should submit to them. In the heats of summer I will stand in line to vote for my favorite politician who has vowed to increase oil exports from the arab states. Oh oil, How I love you. Oh Politicians! How I worship you.

Journalists:

The arbitrers of truth. The fighters of justice. They talk about the most pressing issues of the day. Did the politicians steal money? Or did the politicians do a photoshoot in the park? Of course they work on their own without any external or higher oversight. They are free to write what they want. And they write on their own websites and are completly individually supported. They love the common man. They never help start wars or sit quite when they are bombing places to dust. Oh no, they are the most ethical and highly regarded heroes of our era. Love the journalists. Especially the foreign educated ones, they always bring something special to the table.

Teachers:

Oh, isn’t it wonderful that I get told what to do and what to say and how to talk and write from infancy to middle age. Oh how perfect the teachers are. How full of energy, knowledge and passion. They know things that I can only dream to think of. They teach us algebra. They make us understand things. Teachers want us to learn so much. They tell us to go outside from what they have to offer and to branch out into our own. They don’t care about this or that. Just us. They want the best for us. They are ideally the perfect human beings. How I love my teachers. Literally love them.

Police:

Love the police coming straight from the heavens up. How I love middle aged men and women who are so fit and strong and so stern in their stature that they can do as they please. They love us more than our parents. Just listen to them. How sweet their tongues are. How beautiful their spirit. How calm they are in matters that require seriousness. How reasonable they all get. The most brave and I salute all Police.

The enlightenment hasn’t been a disaster for the human race. It has just been spectacularly sad. It doesn’t take reading Rousseau to reason that what is promised never gets delivered and thinking for others isn’t a virtue, just plain hypocrisy charaded as freedom.
